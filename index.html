Company Overview

On Will GmbH, headquartered in Moers, Germany, was founded by Shu Wang. The company specializes in logistics and transportation, warehousing services, import & export trade, and the restaurant business. Our operations cover both local and international markets, aiming to provide clients with efficient and seamless cross-sector solutions.

02

Mission & Values

Our mission is to deliver efficient, secure, and reliable cross-sector services.
At On Will GmbH, we put customers first, focusing on long-term partnerships and value creation. Through innovation and professionalism, we empower our clients to meet the challenges of globalization and achieve sustainable growth.
